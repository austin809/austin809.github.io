April 13, 2014 | Inline vs. Block Elements

One time a p tag and a span tag were waiting to checkout at the grocery store. The p tag seemed to be in a giant hurry, and tried to cut the span tag. The span tag said: "No cutting, get inline!" to which the p tag responded: "stop it! You can't block me!"

Okay all lame jokes aside, today's blog is all about the difference between inline and block elements. Some elements are inline - they naturaly integrate themselves with the elements around them, where as others are block elements, they are separated out from the other items on the page nearby.
